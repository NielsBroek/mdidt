Technological Applications

Virtual Reality

As explained above digital twins may be represented in a virtual-, augmented-, or mixed reality. This page will focus on the former which means it will focus on digital twins in a completely virtual environment. While augmented- and mixed reality are a mixture of the digital world and our physical world, virtual reality withholds a completely digital world. This can be visualized through virtual glasses like the Microsoft Hololens but also on a computer screen. This means digital twins represented in such a reality may also be visualized through these hardware.
